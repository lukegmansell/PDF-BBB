Comprehensive visual identity rules for the COTCP platform. This document defines the structural and aesthetic tokens required to maintain a consistent, premium enterprise experience.

Usage Rules

check_circle DO

  • Use Muted Gold primarily for high-impact statistics and primary calls to action.
  • Maintain large corner radii (16px/rounded-xl) for all main container cards to soften the dark theme.

cancel DON'T

  • Avoid solid gold outlines on large containers; use dashed borders only for specific 'drop zones'.
  • Do not use stark, high-contrast pure white (#FFFFFF) for body text; rely on Light Grey to reduce eye strain.
